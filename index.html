About 48% of women in STEM jobs report discrimination in the recruitment and hiring process. Black and Hispanic women, who majored in computer science or engineering, are less likely to be hired into a tech role than their white counterparts. Also, 39% of women view gender bias as a primary reason for not being offered a promotion. Finally, 66% of women report that there is no clear path forward for them in their career at their current companies.
